End-to-End Ticket Resolution — 9 Stages

From participant inquiry to closed DevRev ticket, including the final DevRev AI → n8n webhook that seals the loop.
1
DevRev Ticket
Participant raises an inquiry. Ticket created & webhook fires.
2
n8n Decomposition
AI splits the ticket into distinct inquiries & topics.
3
Required Data
KB API returns the fields each inquiry needs.
4
ForUsBots RPA
Scrapes portal & returns participant data to n8n.
5
Pinecone Search
Filtered semantic retrieval by record_keeper & topic.
6
LLM Routing
Gemini / GPT generate the structured outcome response.
7
kb_bundle_v1
n8n consolidates responses & sends to DevRev AI Agent.
8
DevRev AI → n8n
AI crafts reply & posts final-handling webhook to n8n.
9
Ticket Injected & Closed
n8n writes reply + internal notes into DevRev & closes the ticket.
